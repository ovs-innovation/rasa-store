## TASK 4 — FAQ PAGE

**Ordering & Delivery**
1. **How do I place an order on Farmacykart?**  
   Simply browse our categories, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout. For prescription medicines, you will be prompted to upload a valid doctor's prescription before checkout.
2. **Where do you currently deliver?**  
   We primarily serve Ghaziabad and surrounding areas. Enter your PIN code on the product page to check delivery availability.
3. **What are your delivery timeframes?**  
   Standard grocery orders are usually delivered within 24 hours. Medicine delivery times may vary based on prescription verification but are typically fulfilled within 24-48 hours. 

**Prescriptions & Medicines**
4. **Is a prescription mandatory for buying medicines?**  
   Yes, for any drugs listed under Schedule H, H1, or X, a valid prescription from a registered medical practitioner is mandatory by Indian law.
5. **How do I upload my prescription?**  
   You can easily upload a clear photo or PDF of your prescription directly on the product page or during the checkout process.
6. **Are all the medicines genuine?**  
   Absolutely. We strictly partner with licensed pharmacies and verified distributors. *(Always consult your doctor before taking any medication).*

**Payments & Refunds**
7. **What payment methods do you accept?**  
   We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), Credit/Debit Cards, NetBanking, and Cash on Delivery (COD).
8. **How do I cancel my order?**  
   You can cancel your order from the 'My Orders' section before it is dispatched. Once dispatched, standard orders cannot be canceled.
9. **When will I get my refund for a canceled order?**  
   Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days directly to your original payment method (UPI/Card). For COD orders, refunds (if applicable for returns) will be credited to your bank account.

**Returns & Policies**
10. **What is your return policy?**  
    Groceries and general wellness items can be returned within 3 days if damaged. However, per Indian regulations, prescription medicines and temperature-sensitive drugs cannot be returned once delivered.
11. **I received a damaged/wrong product. What should I do?**  
    Please raise a complaint within 24 hours of delivery through our 'Contact Us' page or WhatsApp support, attaching a photo of the damaged item.

**General Questions**
12. **Do you have a mobile app?**  
    Currently, our platform is fully mobile-optimized for your browser. [Update this if an app is available].
13. **Are you properly licensed?**  
    Yes, Farmacykart complies with all Indian IT Act, Drug & Cosmetics Act, and FSSAI regulations. All our vendors hold valid trade and drug licenses.
14. **How do you handle cold-chain medicines (like insulin)?**  
    Temperature-sensitive medicines are packed in specialized cooling boxes to maintain their efficacy during transit. 
15. **Is my personal data safe?**  
    Yes, we strictly adhere to the DPDP Act 2023. Your health data and personal information are encrypted and never sold to third parties.

## TASK 8 — RETURN & REFUND POLICY

**Non-Returnable Items**  
In compliance with Indian healthcare regulations, the following items **cannot** be returned once delivered:
* Prescription medicines
* Temperature-sensitive items (cold-chain drugs)
* Opened personal care and hygiene products
* Baby food and formula

**Eligible Return Conditions**  
You can request a return within 3 days of delivery ONLY if:
* The product is damaged or tampered with upon delivery.
* The product is past its expiry date.
* The wrong product was delivered.

**How to Raise a Return Request**  
1. Go to 'My Orders' and select the order.
2. Click on 'Return/Exchange' and select the reason.
3. Upload a clear photograph of the product and invoice.
4. Our team will review the request within 24 hours.

**Refund Timeline**  
Approved refunds will be processed to the original payment method:
* **UPI/Wallets:** 1-2 business days.
* **Credit/Debit Cards:** 5-7 business days.
* **Cash on Delivery:** A secure payout link will be sent via SMS/Email to transfer funds to your bank account within 3-5 days.

**Exchange Policy**  
Exchanges are only allowed for damaged or incorrect items. We will arrange a free reverse pickup and deliver the replacement product.
